## Part 2: 5 Automations That Save 10+ Hours/Week

### Automation 1: Instant Lead Follow-Up
**Time saved: 2-3 hours/week**

When a new lead fills out a form or calls, they get an immediate text + email within 60 seconds — even at 2 AM.

**Setup:**
- Trigger: New form submission or missed call
- Action 1: Send personalized SMS "Hey {name}, thanks for reaching out! We got your message and will be in touch within the hour."
- Action 2: Send email with your info + booking link
- Action 3: Notify your team in Slack/Discord

**Tools needed:** GoHighLevel (or Zapier + your CRM)

---

### Automation 2: Review Request Sequence
**Time saved: 1-2 hours/week**

After completing a job or appointment, automatically request a Google review via SMS.

**Setup:**
- Trigger: Job marked complete / appointment finished
- Wait: 2 hours
- Action: Send SMS "Hey {name}, thanks for choosing {business}! If you had a great experience, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? {review_link}"
- Wait: 3 days
- Action: If no review, send follow-up

**Tools needed:** GoHighLevel or Make

---

### Automation 3: Social Media Content Pipeline
**Time saved: 3-4 hours/week**

Generate a week of social media posts in 30 minutes using AI, then schedule them.

**Setup:**
- Step 1: Ask ChatGPT "Create 7 social media posts for a [your business type] in [your city]. Each post should have a hook, value, and call-to-action."
- Step 2: Copy the posts into Canva to create graphics
- Step 3: Schedule all 7 at once using GoHighLevel social planner or Buffer

**Tools needed:** ChatGPT + Canva + scheduler

---

### Automation 4: Appointment Reminders
**Time saved: 1-2 hours/week**

No-shows cost money. Automated reminders cut no-shows by 50%+.

**Setup:**
- 24 hours before: SMS "Reminder: You have an appointment tomorrow at {time}. Reply C to confirm or R to reschedule."
- 2 hours before: SMS "Your appointment is in 2 hours. See you at {address}!"
- If no confirmation: Auto-alert your team

**Tools needed:** GoHighLevel (built-in) or any CRM with SMS

---

### Automation 5: Invoice & Payment Reminders
**Time saved: 1-2 hours/week**

Stop chasing payments manually.

**Setup:**
- When invoice is 3 days overdue: Send friendly reminder email
- When 7 days overdue: Send SMS reminder
- When 14 days overdue: Send final notice + alert your team
- When paid: Send thank-you message

**Tools needed:** Make/Zapier + your invoicing tool (QuickBooks, Stripe, etc.)

## Part 4: 20 AI Prompts for Local Business

### Marketing Copy
1. "Write a Google Business description for a [business type] in [city] that focuses on [key differentiator]."
2. "Create 5 Facebook ad headlines for a [business type] offering [service]. Target audience: [describe audience]."
3. "Write a follow-up email to a lead who asked about [service] but hasn't booked yet. Keep it friendly, not pushy."

### Customer Communication
4. "Write a professional response to this 2-star review: '[paste review]'. Acknowledge their concern and offer to make it right."
5. "Draft a text message to remind a customer about their upcoming appointment tomorrow at [time]."
6. "Write an email to past customers announcing our new [service/product]. Include a special offer for returning customers."

### Social Media
7. "Create 7 Instagram post ideas for a [business type] for this week. Include a mix of tips, behind-the-scenes, and customer features."
8. "Write a LinkedIn post about [industry trend] from the perspective of a local [business type] owner."
9. "Generate 3 short-form video script ideas (30 seconds each) for a [business type] to post on TikTok/Reels."

### Operations
10. "Create a standard operating procedure (SOP) for handling new customer inquiries at a [business type]. Include response templates."
11. "Write a job posting for a [position] at a [business type]. Emphasize [key benefits of working here]."
12. "Create a customer satisfaction survey with 5-7 questions for a [business type]."

### Strategy
13. "Analyze this competitor's website [URL] and suggest 3 ways we can differentiate our [business type]."
14. "What are the top 5 local SEO keywords for a [business type] in [city]?"
15. "Create a 90-day marketing plan for a [business type] with a budget of $[amount]/month."

### Business Planning
16. "What are the most common pain points for customers of a [business type]? Create content ideas to address each one."
17. "Suggest 3 partnership opportunities for a [business type] in [city] that could drive referrals."
18. "Create a pricing comparison table for [service] that shows our value vs. competitors."
19. "Write a quarterly business review outline for a [business type] — what metrics should we track?"
20. "What AI tools should a [business type] with [X] employees adopt first? Prioritize by ROI and ease of implementation."
