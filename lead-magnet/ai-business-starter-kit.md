## Part 2: 5 Automations That Save 10+ Hours/Week

### Automation 1: Auto-Reply to Leads
**Tool:** GoHighLevel
**Time saved:** 3-5 hours/week

Set up an automated text + email sequence that fires the moment someone fills out your form or calls.

```
Lead fills form → Instant text: "Hey [Name], thanks for reaching out! 
I'll get back to you within the hour. — [Your Business]"
→ Wait 1 hour → Email with your best offer
→ Wait 2 days → Follow-up text if no response
```

### Automation 2: Review Request Machine
**Tool:** GoHighLevel
**Time saved:** 2-3 hours/week

After completing a job, automatically text the customer:
```
"Thanks for choosing us, [Name]! Could you leave us a quick review? 
It takes 30 seconds: [Google Review Link]"
```

### Automation 3: Social Media on Autopilot
**Tool:** ChatGPT + Postiz/Buffer
**Time saved:** 3-4 hours/week

1. Use ChatGPT to write 30 social posts in 30 minutes
2. Load them into Postiz
3. Post automatically every day for a month

### Automation 4: Competitor Monitoring
**Tool:** Firecrawl + Make.com
**Time saved:** 2-3 hours/week

Automatically scrape competitor websites weekly and email yourself a summary of what changed.

### Automation 5: Customer FAQ Bot
**Tool:** GoHighLevel + ChatGPT
**Time saved:** 2-4 hours/week

Train a chatbot on your website and FAQ. It answers common questions 24/7 so you don't have to.
