# Your AI Plug — Community & Courses

Monetization hub for Your AI Plug: lead magnets, landing pages, courses, and community assets.

## Structure

```
lead-magnet/          — Free PDF content (AI Business Starter Kit)
landing-page/         — Email capture + GHL affiliate pages
courses/              — Course outlines and content for School platform
social/               — 30-day content calendar and social assets
```

## Quick Setup

1. Replace `PASTE_GHL_WEBHOOK_URL_HERE` in `landing-page/index.html` with your GHL webhook
2. Replace `YOUR_AFFILIATE_ID` in `landing-page/ghl-affiliate.html` with your GHL affiliate link
3. Deploy to Cloudflare Pages (or Netlify)
