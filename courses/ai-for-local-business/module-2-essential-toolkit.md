## Lesson 1: The Only 5 Tools You Need to Know About

There are thousands of AI tools out there. 95% of them won't help your business. Here are the ones that will.

### Tool 1: ChatGPT or Claude (AI Assistant)

**What it is:** A website where you type questions or requests and get intelligent answers back. Like texting a really smart assistant.

**What you use it for:**
- Writing emails, social posts, descriptions, proposals
- Brainstorming ideas
- Researching topics
- Editing and improving things you've already written
- Creating templates you can reuse

**Cost:** Free tier available. $20/month for the paid version (worth it).

**Best for:** Everyone. This is your starting point if you use nothing else.

**Real example:**
> "Write me a Facebook post for my plumbing business about the importance of water heater maintenance before winter. Keep it friendly, not salesy. Include a call to action to book a free inspection."

That's it. Type that in, get a polished post in 10 seconds. Edit it to sound like you, post it. Done.

### Tool 2: Canva with Magic Studio (Design + AI)

**What it is:** A design tool that now has AI built in. Makes social media graphics, flyers, business cards, and more.

**What you use it for:**
- Social media graphics (already sized for each platform)
- Before/after photos with text overlay
- Promotional flyers
- Business cards
- Simple video content

**Cost:** Free tier available. $13/month for Pro (worth it for the templates and brand kit).

**Best for:** Any business that needs visual content — which is every business.

**Why it's on this list:** Canva's AI features now let you describe what you want and it designs it. "Create an Instagram post promoting 20% off HVAC tune-ups with a summer theme." It builds it. You tweak the colors and text. 5 minutes instead of 50.

### Tool 3: GoHighLevel (CRM + Automation)

**What it is:** An all-in-one platform that handles your customer relationships, automates follow-ups, builds websites and funnels, and manages your online reputation.

**What you use it for:**
- Storing customer information (CRM)
- Automatically following up with leads via text and email
- Building landing pages and simple websites
- Collecting and managing online reviews
- Appointment scheduling
- Running email and text marketing campaigns

**Cost:** $97/month directly, or FREE with a Your AI Plug Starter membership ($27/month).

**Best for:** Any business that gets leads and needs to follow up with them. Especially service businesses.

**Why it matters:** Most businesses lose 50-80% of their leads because they don't follow up fast enough. GHL automates that entire process. A lead fills out a form on your website at 10pm? They get a personalized text in 30 seconds, an email in 2 minutes, and a follow-up the next morning — all without you touching anything.

### Tool 4: ManyChat or Chatfuel (Chat Automation)

**What it is:** Tools that let you build automated conversations for Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, and your website chat.

**What you use it for:**
- Answering common questions automatically
- Capturing leads from social media comments
- Booking appointments through chat
- Sending promotions to people who message you
- Building a "DM me KEYWORD" system for Instagram

**Cost:** Free tier available. $15-25/month for business features.

**Best for:** Businesses active on Instagram or Facebook. Anyone getting DMs they can't keep up with.

**Real example:**
> Someone comments "INFO" on your Instagram post about your services. ManyChat automatically DMs them with a welcome message, asks what they're interested in, and sends them to your booking page. You didn't touch anything. That person just became a lead.

### Tool 5: Loom or ScreenPal (Quick Video)

**What it is:** Tools that record your screen and face so you can create quick videos — tutorials, walkthroughs, explanations.

**What you use it for:**
- Recording quick how-to videos for customers
- Creating video responses instead of long emails
- Training employees on processes
- Recording video testimonials or case studies
- Making social media content (screen recordings with your face)

**Cost:** Free tier available. $15/month for business features.

**Best for:** Any business that explains the same things repeatedly. "Let me just show you" is more powerful than writing it out.

**Why it's here:** Video content gets 10x the engagement of text on social media. And most of your competitors aren't doing it. A 2-minute video recorded on your phone or laptop beats a perfectly designed graphic every time.

## Lesson 3: Setting Up Your First Tool (Step by Step)

Let's set up the most important one: **GoHighLevel through Your AI Plug.**

This handles your customer database, follow-up automation, review collection, and appointment scheduling — all in one place. It's the backbone of everything else.

### Step 1: Get Your Account

1. Go to [youraiplug.com/pricing](https://youraiplug.com/pricing)
2. Choose the Starter plan ($27/month)
3. Complete checkout
4. You'll receive your GoHighLevel login within 24 hours

### Step 2: Set Up Your Business Profile

Once you log in:

1. **Settings → Company** — Add your business name, logo, phone number, email, and address
2. **Settings → Business Profile** — Add your hours, website, and social media links
3. **Settings → Phone Numbers** — Get your tracking number (this lets you see which marketing is working)

### Step 3: Import Your Contacts

You probably have customer information scattered everywhere — phone contacts, old spreadsheets, email lists. Let's consolidate.

1. **Contacts → Import** — Upload a CSV file of your contacts
2. Include at minimum: Name, Phone, Email
3. Add tags to organize them (e.g., "past customer", "lead", "vip")

**Don't have a CSV?** Export from your phone, your email program, or just type them in manually for your top 50 customers to start.

### Step 4: Set Up Your First Automation

This is where the magic happens. Let's create a simple "new lead" automation:

1. **Automation → Create Workflow**
2. Choose "Start from Scratch"
3. Set the trigger: "When a form is submitted" or "When a tag is added"
4. Add your first action: **Send SMS**
   - Write a friendly message: "Hi {first_name}! Thanks for reaching out to [Your Business]. I'll get back to you shortly, but in the meantime, here's a link to schedule a time that works for you: [your booking link]"
5. Add second action (wait 5 minutes): **Send Email**
   - A brief email introducing yourself and your services
6. Add third action (wait 24 hours, if no reply): **Send Follow-up SMS**
   - "Hi {first_name}, just checking in! Did you have any questions I can help with?"

That's it. Now every new lead gets an instant response, a follow-up email, and a next-day check-in. Without you lifting a finger.

### Step 5: Set Up Your Reputation Tool

1. **Reputation → Settings**
2. Connect your Google Business Profile
3. Create a review request template: "Hi {first_name}, thank you for choosing [Your Business]! Would you mind leaving us a quick review? It really helps us out: {review_link}"
4. Set up automation to send this after every completed job/appointment

**Why this matters:** Businesses with 50+ Google reviews get 3-5x more calls than businesses with fewer than 10. Automating review requests is the single easiest way to grow your local presence.
