## Content Batching Schedule

**Every Sunday:** Batch-create the next week's content
1. Write all captions with ChatGPT (30 min)
2. Create graphics with Canva (45 min)
3. Record 2-3 short videos (60 min)
4. Schedule everything in Postiz (30 min)

**Total time:** ~2.5 hours/week for daily content across all platforms

## Email Newsletter Template

**Subject line formula:** [Number] + [Benefit] + [Timeframe]
- "3 AI tools that saved me 10 hours this week"
- "5 automations every business needs in 2026"
- "This week in AI: what matters for your business"

**Structure:**
1. Personal story or insight (2-3 sentences)
2. Main value: tip, tutorial, or tool review
3. Community highlight (member win or question)
4. CTA: join community / upgrade / share
